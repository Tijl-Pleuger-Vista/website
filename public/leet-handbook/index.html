Platform Veilig Ondernemen (PVO) Limburg

PVO helps entrepreneurs in the province of Limburg to make themselves more resilient to various forms of crime

We achieve this with safety enhancers on various themes

This includes cybercrime, robberies, threats, fraudulent rental or rental, theft, money laundering, etc

PVO Limburg collaborates with police, municipalities, trade organizations and entrepreneurs at regional and provincial level

This is how we identify developments in the region and tailor our approach
